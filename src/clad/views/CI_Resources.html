Resources

In order to help understand what climate change will mean for you and to help plan for adaptation to climate change, we provide a range of case studies, tools, links and reports. The resources available through these pages are sourced from a variety of sources.

Case Studies

Tools
  • BACLIAT: Business Areas Climate Assessment Tool

    The UKCIP BACLIAT tool has been designed as a workshop tool that provides a means of raising awareness of climate change and the implications of its impacts and adaptation at either a company or a sectoral level.
