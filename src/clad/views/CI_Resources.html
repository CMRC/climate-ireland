Resources

In order to help understand what climate change will mean for you and to help plan for adaptation to climate change, we provide a range of case studies, tools, links and reports.

  • The Case studies section provides examples of climate change adaptation in practice. Case studies cover adaptation actions across a range of sectors and as part a of both a planned programme of adaptation or as a response to a particular event.

  • The Tools section provide a means of implementing adaptation allowing you to go through the adaptation process from determining your vulnerabilities to climate change, your key climate risks, and developing adaptation plans.

  • The Links and Reports section provide a diverse range of information on climate change and adaptation.

Case Studies

Coastal

Towards a more balanced management of a harbour through a Local Authority-Academic Couplet, Cork, Ireland

This case study examines the steps taken towards achieving more balanced management of a multi-use harbour: Cork Harbour, Ireland. It includes the management of climate change impacts and safeguarding of coasts/coastal systems, through the establishment of a strategic alliance (couplet) between the local authority and multi-disciplinary academic experts. This innovative relationship resulted in teh adaptation of an Integrated Harbour Management Strategy set up with the concensus of stakeholders, and a strengthening link between science and policy at the local level.
