Adaptation Information at your fingertips

It is now clear that climate change is already underway and the situation is urgent. In order to deal with worst consequences of climate change, adaptation is now required.

'Climate Ireland' will help you to plan for adaptation to climate change by raising your awareness about climate change and adaptation, providing you with the information and data that you will require to plan for adaptation and through the provision of the necessary guidane and tools to employ this information and data in your planning.

What do you want to do?
  • Learn more about climate change
  • Learn more about adaptation to climate change
  • Explore climatic information and data
  • Explore how climate change might impact upon your sector
  • Explore a range of resources to help you develop your adaptation plan
