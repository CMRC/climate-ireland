Welcome to Climate Ireland

"Adaptation to climate change refers to adjustment in natural or human systems in response to actual or expected climatic stimuli or their effects, which moderates harm or exploits beneficial opportunities" (IPCC, 2001)

Climate is changing and adaptation to climate change is now required to offset the worst effects of climate change. 'Climate Ireland' provides a web-based resources of climatic information and data to facilitate sectoral and regional decision makers in adapting to climate change.

Climate Ireland will:

  • Raise awareness about Climate Change and Adaptation by providing the information required to understand what climate change is, what it means for us and how we can adapt to the consequences of our changing climate;
  • Provide the information and data necessary for planning for climate change
  • Provide decision support for planning for adaptation to climate change including tools, methods and frameworks to help you understand current climate and plan for future climate
