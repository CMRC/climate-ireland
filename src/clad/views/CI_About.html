Adaptation Information at your fingertips

It is now clear that climate change is already underway and that the situation is urgent. In order to deal with the worst consequences of climate change, anticipatory and proactive adaptation is now required.

'Climate Ireland' will help you to plan for adaptation to climate change by raising your awareness and increasing your understanding of climate change and adaptation, providing you with the required information and data to plan for adaptation to climate change, and through the provision of the necessary guidance and tools, enabling you to effectively employ this information and data in your planning.

What do you want to do?

  • Learn more about climate change.
  • Learn more about adaptation to climate change.
  • Explore climatic information and data.
  • Explore how climate change might impact upon your sector.
  • Explore a range of resources to help you develop your adaptation plan.
